    #TuskYou

    #TuskYou is a movement that aims to put a stop to the trade by encouraging everyone to go against it in an easy but powerful way. Change your profile photo on social media by raising two middle fingers in front of your face and mimicking the shape of the elephant tusks to mock ivory buyers and unite everyone against the poaching of elephants.

    By joining the cause, you can be part of a global protest, spreading the message to pressure buyers to rethink the purchase of ivory.
